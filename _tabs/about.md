# About Me

Welcome to my academic portfolio.

My name is **Abdul Rehman**, and I am a Computer Engineering student. This website serves as a record of my learning journey, technical growth, and academic experiences throughout my first two semesters. Following the guidance of **Dr. Bilal Ahmad**, I started documenting my experiences through blog posts to build a professional digital footprint and reflect on my progress as a student.

Through this portfolio, I share my experiences in Programming Fundamentals, Database Systems, Machine Learning concepts, project development, GitHub portfolio creation, and various challenges I faced during my academic journey.

---

## About My Mentor

A significant part of my learning journey has been influenced by **Dr. Bilal Ahmad**, who taught us Programming Fundamentals and Database Systems. His teaching approach focuses on understanding concepts rather than memorization. He frequently connects classroom concepts with real-world applications of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning.

One of the most valuable lessons I learned from him is that strong foundations in programming and data management are essential for building intelligent systems. His emphasis on working with meaningful and high-quality datasets inspired me to explore Machine Learning and data-driven technologies.
