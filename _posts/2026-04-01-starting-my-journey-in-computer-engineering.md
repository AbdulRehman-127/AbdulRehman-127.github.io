# Starting My Journey in Computer Engineering

## Introduction to My First Step in Computer Engineering

Beginning my journey in Computer Engineering was one of the most important phases of my academic life. When I first entered this field, I had only a basic idea of programming and computer systems. Everything felt new, complex, and slightly overwhelming because I was entering a completely logical world that required a different way of thinking.

At the start of my semester, I was introduced to my instructor **Dr. Bilal Ahmad**, who was teaching Programming Fundamentals and Database Systems at UET Lahore, Faisalabad Campus. From the very first lecture, Dr. Bilal Ahmad focused on developing logical thinking rather than memorization. He explained that computer engineering is not about copying code but about understanding how problems are solved step by step.

## Learning Programming Fundamentals and Early Struggles

In the beginning, Programming Fundamentals was very challenging for me. Even basic concepts like variables, loops, and conditions were confusing. Writing my first programs was difficult because small mistakes created errors that I could not understand at first.

However, Dr. Bilal Ahmad guided us to treat errors as part of learning. He encouraged us not to fear mistakes but to analyze them carefully. This mindset slowly changed my approach toward programming. Instead of giving up, I started spending more time understanding why an error occurred.

With practice, I realized that programming is not about writing code quickly but about thinking logically before writing anything. Debugging slowly became an interesting process instead of a frustrating one.

---

## Teaching Approach of Dr. Bilal Ahmad and Real-World Thinking

One of the most valuable parts of my learning experience was the teaching style of **Dr. Bilal Ahmad**. He did not only focus on theory but also explained how programming concepts are used in real-world applications.

He often related Programming Fundamentals and Database Systems to advanced fields like artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data-driven systems. Although we were studying basic concepts, his explanations helped us understand the bigger picture of computer engineering.

Dr. Bilal Ahmad also has expertise in AI, ML, and DL model training, and he sometimes shared examples of real datasets and practical problems. This made us realize that the foundation we are building today will be useful in future advanced technologies.

---

## Personal Growth, Challenges, and Future Direction

As the semester progressed, I faced many challenges. Some topics required repeated study before I could fully understand them. There were moments of confusion and frustration, but continuous practice helped me improve.

Dr. Bilal Ahmad always encouraged consistency and revision. His guidance helped me build discipline in my studies. Slowly, I started improving my logical thinking and problem-solving ability.

Looking back, I can clearly see how much I have grown. I started as a beginner with confusion and slowly developed confidence in programming concepts. This journey has built a strong foundation for my future studies in Computer Engineering.

I now feel more motivated to continue learning advanced topics in upcoming semesters, especially Database Systems and programming-related fields.
